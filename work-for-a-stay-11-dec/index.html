Everyone loves staycations. Now here’s your chance to enjoy a complimentary 1-night hotel stay, and get some hands-on experience of what it’s like to work in the Business of Happiness.


Sign up for Work For A Stay, and try your hand at essential
day-to-day hotel operations like F&B, housekeeping, and front office in one of 15 participating hotels across Singapore.


At the end of the 10-day paid immersion program, you’ll be rewarded with a free night’s stay at the hotel you’ve been attached to.
What’s more, you might even earn the opportunity to embark on a promising new career in the Business of Happiness.


Registration opens on 20th November 2017.

Amara Singapore • Amara Sanctuary Resort Sentosa
Mandarin Orchard Singapore • Pan Pacific Hotel Singapore
Grand Copthorne Waterfront Hotel • Copthorne King’s Hotel Singapore
M Hotel Singapore • M Social Singapore • Orchard Hotel Singapore
Studio M Hotel Singapore • Oasia Hotel Downtown • Oasia Hotel Novena
